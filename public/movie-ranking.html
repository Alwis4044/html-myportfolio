The Best Movies According to Alwis

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Top Gun: Maverick

A great movie for jet lovers. I love the F-18 raptor jets used in the movie.

Jailer

A really action-packed thriller movie. The entry scene of Rajnikanth sir is my favourite part.

Transformers(2007)

A sci-fi movie which was way ahead of its time. I really loved the stunning vfx of this movie.
